# Phase 3 — Funding Path

## The one-sentence version
With a live SaaS product, paying customers, and placement metrics, raise a proper round in 2027 with a very specific pitch.

## The narrative we'll tell

> Hiring operations are one of the last large white-collar workflows still running almost entirely on manual labor and Excel — inside agencies and inside every company that hires. We've solved it. Here are the numbers.

Not a prototype. Not a demo. **A live SaaS product with paying customers and measurable outcomes.**

## What the numbers will show (when we have them)

- Revenue generated or commission uplift **per recruiter** post-automation
- Reduction in **time-per-placement**
- **Cost-of-running-the-system vs ROI** ratio
- Number of anchor clients live
- Retention / NPS from those clients
- Placement rate comparison to industry baseline

## Who this goes to
- Sector-focused VCs who understand recruitment or HR-tech
- Operators-turned-investors with ex-Workday / ex-iCIMS / ex-Greenhouse background
- Angels with recruitment-agency operator experience
- Later-stage opportunistic funds if the numbers are loud

## What we're NOT doing in Phase 3
- Raising on a deck alone
- Raising before retention data exists
- Raising for a pivot — this round is capital to scale what's already working

## What funding unlocks
- Proper engineering team (dedicated back-end + front-end + ML)
- Dedicated sales motion into mid-market HR teams
- Multi-tenant infrastructure hardening
- Compliance certifications (SOC 2, GDPR, SG PDPA)
- Proper product design function (not just Shoham in the evenings)

## What needs to be true before raising
- [ ] 3+ paying agency customers past 6 months
- [ ] 1+ mid-market HR team pilot
- [ ] Renewal or retention signal (contracts rolling over, not just won)
- [ ] Gross margin visible — not just top-line revenue
- [ ] A narrative of the next 12 months that is **execution**, not hope

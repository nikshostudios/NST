# Shoham

## Role in Niksho
Co-founder. Design, product taste, front-end, and the operator of this vault.

## What Shoham does
- Designs the user experience and UI for the web app.
- Owns the visual identity of Niksho as a product and eventually as a brand.
- Runs the second brain (this vault) day-to-day — reading raw sources, capturing clippings, promoting concepts to Atlas.
- Works evenings on product design since the main day is spent on other things.
- Partner to Nikhil on every meaningful product decision.

## Working relationship with Nikhil
- Nikhil is the builder — backend, agents, schemas, Railway deploys.
- Shoham is the product sense and the story — what should this feel like, how does it land with a user, how do we explain it.
- Decisions go one way when they're clear, both ways when they're not. No silent vetoes.

## Preferences
- Direct feedback over diplomatic feedback.
- Prose over bullet points for anything that needs to be read, not skimmed.
- Looks at the actual output, not just the plan. "Show me the rendered thing."
- Prefers one strong recommendation with reasoning over three weak options.

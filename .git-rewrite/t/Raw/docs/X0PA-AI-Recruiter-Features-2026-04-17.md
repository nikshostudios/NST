# X0PA AI — Full Feature Breakdown

> Source: sales deck titled "AI Recruiter Platform — Core Features" plus "X0PA ROOM Platform — Video Interviews & Assessments" plus "Agentic AI Experience — Features". Extracted from PPTX uploaded 2026-04-17.

## Platform 1: AI Recruiter

### 1. Job Description Generator
- Unlimited variants — generate multiple versions tailored to different needs
- Customisable tone, format, and length — adjust voice, structure, and detail level to suit brand
- Bias-free language — ensures inclusive and neutral phrasing to attract diverse talent
- Data-driven optimisation — fine-tuned using historic hiring data for relevance and performance

### 2. Bias Checker
- Bias detection — scans JDs for gender, race, ethnicity, and other bias indicators
- Language clean-up — flags profane words, slang, and non-professional phrases
- Inclusive language suggestions — recommends neutral, inclusive alternatives

### 3. Skill Taxonomy
- 35,000+ standardised skills database
- Integrated frameworks: SkillsFuture, ESCO, and additional global standards
- Unified skill mapping — aligns job roles, candidate profiles, and training needs
- Dynamic skill tagging — automatically tags and categorises skills from resumes and JDs
- Supports skill-based hiring — enables precise matching and upskilling paths

### 4. Job Approvals
- Multi-layered approval flows — supports complex, multi-step processes
- Unlimited approval levels
- Org chart-based routing — aligns approval workflows with organisational hierarchy
- Customisable rules — define conditions and approvers by role, department, or location
- Real-time status tracking with instant updates and notifications

### 5. Job Distribution
- Global job board integration — distribute to 3,000+ external job boards worldwide
- Automated LinkedIn publishing — push jobs to LinkedIn with no manual effort
- One-click multi-channel posting
- Custom distribution rules — target boards by role, location, or industry
- Performance tracking — monitor application sources to optimise distribution strategy

### 6. Job Persona (DISC Match)
- Job-specific DISC match — identifies the ideal DISC personality type for each JD
- Personality-job fit — matches candidates' DISC profiles to job requirements

### 7. Soft Skills Mapping
- Core and good-to-have soft skills identification per role
- Role-specific skill mapping — matches candidate soft skills with job requirements
- Data-driven soft skill insights — leverages historical data to fine-tune expectations

### 8. Pre-Screening Questions
- Auto-generated questions — creates relevant pre-screening questions automatically
- Knockout questions — critical questions that auto-disqualify candidates
- Auto shortlisting/rejecting — based on knockout answers or pass/fail thresholds
- Essay evaluation — auto-evaluates essay-style responses and assigns scores

### 9. AI Scoring — Suitability Model
- Candidate-to-job fit model — matches on skills, experience, and qualifications
- CV relevance scoring — evaluates CV relevance to JD using AI
- Career progression analysis — assesses career growth and potential for the role
- NLP-based screening — analyses and understands candidate profiles
- Multi-language support — 72+ languages

### 10. AI Scoring — BRIQ Model
- NLP + LLM-based advanced candidate matching
- Smart match highlighting — highlights relevant sentences/phrases in CVs that align with recruiter criteria

### 11. AI Scoring — Persona Fit Model
- Work style fit — evaluates how candidate's work preferences align with job role and team
- Soft skill fit — assesses alignment of soft skills with role requirements and org culture

### 12. AI Scoring — Configurability
- Customisable sub-component scores — toggle on/off any sub-component
- Adjustable weightages — set and customise for individual score components
- Job-level and company-level configuration
- Ethical AI practices — models undergo fairness testing via AI Verify framework
- Flexible scoring model — tailor AI scores to org's unique recruitment needs

### 13. AI Candidate Re-targeting
- Past applicant matching — identifies previous applicants and matches to current open roles
- AI-based scoring and matching for re-targeted candidates
- 3-month window — retargets candidates who applied in the past 3 months
- Streamlined re-engagement — reduces time-to-hire by reconnecting with qualified candidates

### 14. Candidate Sourcing
- Access to 250M+ passive candidates — global pool of passive talent
- Guaranteed LinkedIn links for every candidate sourced
- Email/phone access for a select subset of candidates
- GDPR compliant

### 15. Lotus — CV Parser (Beta)
- Multi-language support — 72+ languages
- High accuracy — 95% accuracy in extracting relevant information
- Image resume support — parses resumes in image format (scanned PDFs)
- Universal format compatibility — PDF, Word, etc.
- Structured JSON output for easy integration

### 16. Recruitment Process Automation
- 90% workflow automation — sourcing, screening, scheduling, and communication
- Human-in-the-loop control — shortlist/reject decisions, interview feedback, and offer negotiation stay human
- Stage-level automation toggle — enable/disable automation at any stage
- Customisable automation rules — define triggers and conditions per workflow
- Efficiency at scale — reduces manual tasks while keeping critical decisions human-led

### 17. Auto Scheduling of Interviews
- Calendar sync — auto-detects availability during business hours
- Auto slot suggestions — sends available time slots to candidates
- One-click scheduling — eliminates back-and-forth
- Smart panel management — supports required and optional panel members
- Integrated invites — auto-generates calendar invites with Zoom, Teams, or Google Meet links

### 18. Data Collection Slots
- Multi-stage data collection — post-screening, post-interview, pre-offer, post-offer, post-hire
- Custom forms — text, files, dates, numbers, dropdowns, multi-select
- Approval workflow — collected data can go through approval, with candidate resubmission
- External system integration — transfers collected data for further processing
- Automated notifications — reminders for candidates to complete or resubmit

### 19. Offer Letter Generation & E-Sign
- DocuSign and Adobe E-Sign integration
- X0PA E-Sign (Beta) — own e-signature solution (launching end of 2025)
- Automated offer generation from candidate data
- Customisable templates

### 20. Persona Matching (DISC Assessment)
- 25-question structured multiple-choice test
- DISC personality matrix — generates profile based on responses
- 7 trait dimensions: Task-Oriented, People-Oriented, Expressive, Deliberate, Fast-Paced, Autonomous, Collaborative
- Behavioural insight mapping — work style and interaction preferences
- Fit prediction — matches personality traits to job demands

### 21. Work Style Fit Matching
- Compatibility scoring — calculates work style fit between candidate and hiring manager or team
- Team fit visualisation — visual overview of candidate alignment with team dynamics
- Hiring insight reports — strengths, friction points, and collaboration styles
- Bias-free evaluation — focuses on behavioural alignment, not background

### 22. Analytics & Reports
- Comprehensive dashboards — real-time recruitment metrics
- Custom reports and graphs
- Excel export
- Custom metrics support — track org-specific KPIs alongside standard metrics
- Insight-driven decisions — optimise hiring strategies and process efficiency

### 23. Integrations & Localisation
- 35+ ATS integrations
- 50+ HRMS integrations
- Enterprise system support: Workday, SAP SuccessFactors, Oracle, Darwinbox
- Custom integration connectors
- API / SFTP-based integration
- Secure login: OAuth, SAML, OpenID, Active Directory, SSO
- Customer domain mapping
- Multi-language support — 72+ languages with 100% feature parity

### 24. Dedicated Candidate Portal
- Personalised candidate experience — track application progress, feedback, communication
- Real-time status updates
- Document upload and management
- Self-service interview scheduling and rescheduling
- Mobile-friendly access

### 25. White-Labelled Experience & Career Sites
- Branded career sites — fully customisable to match company's look and feel
- White-labelled candidate experience — seamless, on-brand journey from application to offer
- Custom domains and themes
- Mobile-optimised design

# Fundraising — 2027 Round Prep

## Goal
Raise a proper round in 2027 on the back of a live SaaS product with paying customers and measurable placement outcomes. Not on a deck. Not on a prototype. On numbers.

Full narrative and what-has-to-be-true in [[Atlas/Business-Model/Phase-3-Funding]].

## Status
**Pre-work.** Not an active push. We don't take investor meetings beyond friendly catch-ups until the Phase 2 pilots produce retention data.

## What this effort tracks
1. The evolving narrative (updated as the product and customer list grows)
2. The target investor list (built from now, not at the last minute)
3. Key metrics we'll show in the deck (tracked in real time, not reconstructed)
4. Artifacts we'll need (financial model, metrics dashboard, customer reference list)
5. Legal and structural prep (entity structure, IP ownership, cap table hygiene)

## The narrative in one sentence
> Hiring operations are one of the last large white-collar workflows still running almost entirely on manual labor and Excel — inside agencies and inside every company that hires. We've solved it. Here are the numbers.

## What needs to be true before we raise
See [[Atlas/Business-Model/Phase-3-Funding#What-needs-to-be-true-before-raising]].

Checklist:
- [ ] 3+ paying agency customers past 6 months
- [ ] 1+ mid-market HR team pilot
- [ ] Renewal or retention signal
- [ ] Gross margin visible
- [ ] 12-month execution narrative (not hope)

## Target investor list (to build)
_(names to be added as we meet people)_

Categories we're looking for:
- Sector-focused VCs (recruitment / HR-tech)
- Operators-turned-investors from Workday, iCIMS, Greenhouse, Bullhorn, Lever
- Angels with recruitment-agency operator backgrounds
- Later-stage opportunistic funds — only if the numbers are loud

## Metrics to track from now
Even though we're not raising yet, we need clean data when the time comes. We can't retrofit this.

- Revenue generated per recruiter (before vs after automation)
- Time-per-placement (days from req to placed)
- Cost-of-running-the-system (Claude tokens + Firecrawl + infra) as % of placement fee
- Recruiter hours saved per day (measured, not guessed)
- NPS / satisfaction from each early customer
- Churn / renewal / logo retention (once Phase 2 starts)

Quarterly rollups live in [[Calendar/Quarterly/2026-Q2]] and future quarters.

## Artifacts to build
- [ ] Financial model (LTV, CAC, unit economics by tenant, cost of Claude per placement)
- [ ] Metrics dashboard (real-time, shareable)
- [ ] Customer reference list (names + willingness to take a call)
- [ ] Demo environment (a polished multi-tenant walkthrough)
- [ ] Deck (last, not first)
- [ ] One-pager (for warm intros)

## Legal / structural prep
- [ ] Confirm entity structure (SG parent? Delaware flip? C-corp?)
- [ ] IP assignment from founders to the company
- [ ] Cap table set up correctly from the start
- [ ] Advisor grants documented
- [ ] Privacy / PDPA / GDPR posture ready for diligence

## What we are deliberately NOT doing
- Taking calls with funds right now, beyond friendly catch-ups.
- Optimising for a specific valuation target.
- Building deck slides before we have numbers that populate them.
- Letting a potential term sheet change our roadmap.

## Open questions
- Lead investor profile: sector VC or generalist?
- When to engage advisors / board observers in the run-up?
- SG vs US entity — which is the right fit for our customer base and our investor base?

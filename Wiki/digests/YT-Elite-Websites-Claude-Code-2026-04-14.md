# The 7 Levels of Building Elite Websites

Source: Tab 3 of [[Raw/transcripts/YT-Transcripts-2026-04-14]]

## Core argument

Design quality from AI follows a progression. Most people stall at Level 1-2 (generic AI output). The leap happens when you bring visual references, inspect source code, combine custom assets, and use external design tools. Taste is not about AI — it's about articulating your taste.

## The 7 levels

**Level 1 — Prompt + generic output.** Just a prompt, no direction. Result: purple gradient template. Escape by learning to write descriptive prompts and building basic design vocabulary.

**Level 2 — Design skills.** Install skills like "Frontend Design" or "UI/UX Pro Max." Gives the AI colour theory, typography, spacing, layout rules. Better than Level 1 but still looks like an AI template. Trap: people stop here.

**Level 3 — Visual director (screenshots).** Bring reference screenshots from Awwwards, Godly.website, Pinterest, Dribbble. AI mimics visual better than written descriptions. Gets you 50% closer but a "vibe gap" remains. Skill to master: evaluate output with a designer's eye, turn critiques into prompts.

**Level 4 — The cloner (understanding code).** Inspect the HTML/CSS/JS of sites you admire (Ctrl+U, "Site Teardown" skill). Ask Claude to explain and clone the design. HTML = bones, CSS = clothes, JS = muscles. Much closer fidelity. Trap: cloning without creative adaptation.

**Level 5 — Customisation (your spin).** Find quality components on 21st.dev, CodePen, Mona. Generate custom hero images (Midjourney) matching your tagline. Add video backgrounds, scroll-tied animations. Iterate 20+ times per section. This is where character emerges through tiny details — micro-interactions, loading states, scroll progress.

**Level 6 — Outside tools (refinement).** Use Stitch (Google, free), Figma, Pencil.dev, Paper.design for visual iteration. Feed designs back to Claude Code. Explore fonts on Google Fonts. Add subtle UX details: loading hesitation for weight, ticker transitions, glassmorphism, counter animations. This is tinkering mode — no shortcuts.

**Level 7 — 3D/WebGL/custom shaders.** Beyond current AI capability for most. Custom WebGL, shaders, video-game-like experiences. Not accessible via prompting yet (as of early 2026).
